## Header

- The header is of 20 bytes.
- A packet contains:
                - IP Version
                - Length
                - Identification Number
                - Flags
                - Protocols
                - Checksum
                - Addresses
                - TTL (Time To Live)


### TTL

- It is known as Time To Live (TTL).
- This is the time after which the packet will be dropped.
